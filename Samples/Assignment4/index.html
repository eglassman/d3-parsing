Scores Across World University Groups

This radar plot shows the average scores of the University Groups based on rankings across categories for the years 2011-2016.

- Select the year from the dropdown to view the appropriate chart.
- Use the configuration options to modify the chart.
- Hover on individual points to see the details.

World University Rankings

This parallel coordinates chart shows the data of the top 30 universities for the years 2011-2016.

- Click the axis to remove the brush.
- Drag around axis names to reorder them.
